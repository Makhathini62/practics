MORE ABOUT MY BLOG AND MY INSPIRATION BEHIND IT


I grew up in a family that loves cooking. So cooking became one of my inheritence which I admire the most.
My love for ccoking started when I was 7 years old but I could not cook as I was still young(according to a perspective of a black parent).
The love and passion grew to a point where I stated learning how to cook. When I started knowing how to cook,
I tried new ideas that were enjoyed by my family. I still love ccoking and ccoking has become my best friend
that only speaks to me through a delicious meal. As I grew up, I also got inspiration to the top chefs
like the likes of Gordon Ramsey, Jammie Oliver, Graham Eliot, Christina Tosi, and our very own chef Coco.
My love and passion for food has made me to want to explore more in ccoking and sharing some ccoking ideas.
