MORE ABOUT PAN-SEARED MEDIUM RARE STEAK

Ingredients:
Any cut of steak
Large grain of sea salt
Ground pepper
Olive oil
Fresh garlic
Fresh thyme
Butter 100g

Method:
1. In a steak, add the sea salt and pepper.
2. In a pan, add the olive oil in a medium heat and add the thyme, butter, and garlic.
3. Sear the sides of the steak for 1 minute each so that it is browned all over.
4. Place the steak in the oven heated to 180 degrees for about 2 to 2 and half minutes per side.
5. Remove the steak from the heat and let it reast for about 5 minutes.
6. Serve the steak with green salad, potato salad, or any starch of your choice.
