MORE ABOUT A SIMPLE CHICKEN ROAST

Ingredients:
1 full chicken
3 1/2tsp chicken spice
3tsp paprika
3tsp tumeric
6Tbsp butter
2tsp salt
2 full lemon squeezed
1tsp black pepper

Method:
1. In a small bowl, add all the dry ingredients and mix them.
2. After a perfect mix, add the squeezed lemon in the mixture and mix together.
3. Separate the chicken skin and the chicken itself using a spoon or finger then add the butter inside for the nice crispy and golden skin.
4. Add the mixture in a bowl as well inside the chicken, inside the skin, and outside the chicken.
5. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees and roast the chicken closed using a closing pan for 45 minutes.
6. Use the lid to create a golden color for 7 1/2 minutes per side with a temperatureof 140 degrees.
7. Cool it down for 15 minutes and enjoy with your family and friends.
8. Serve with potatoes and salad, or rice and salad.
