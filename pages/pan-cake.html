MORE ABOUT PAN CAKES

Ingredients: 2 eggs
1 3/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla essence
2 cups self-raising flour
1/3 cup caster sugar
Butter, for frying
Maple syrup, to serve

Method:
Step 1
Whisk eggs, milk and vanilla together in a jug. Sift flour into a large bowl.
Stir in sugar. Make a well in the centre.
Add milk mixture. Whisk until just combined.
Step 2
Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
Grease pan with butter or spray with cooking oil. Using 1/4 cup mixture per pancake, cook 2 pancakes for 2 minutes or until bubbles appear on surface.
Turn and cook for a further 1-2 minutes or until cooked through.
Transfer to a plate. Cover loosely with foil to keep warm. Repeat with remaining mixture, greasing pan with butter or cooking oil between batches.
Step 3
Serve with maple syrup!!
