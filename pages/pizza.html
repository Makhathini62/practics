MORE ABOUT BREAKFAST PIZZA

Ingredients:
1 lb ball pizza dough
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 cup shredded cheese
1 medium roma tomato
3 oz ham
4 large eggs
2 sliced green onions
1 Tbsp cornmeal
as needed non-stick spray or little oil

Method:
If using frozen pizza dough, allow it to come to room temperature before stretching it into shape (it is much more cooperative at room temp).
While the dough is thawing, slice the tomato, green onion and dice the ham.
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Spray or put a little bit of oil in a perforated pizza pan with non-stick spray.
Stretch the dough into a 16 inch round and place on the pizza pan.
Top the pizza with sauce, half of the cheese, the tomatoes, ham, green onions, eggs and the remaining half of the cheese.
The eggs can either be placed on top whole and raw or pre-scrambled and sprinkled over top.
Bake the pizza for about 15 minutes at 450 degrees or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
